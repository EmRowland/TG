About Mahnaz Roshan

Mahnaz Roshan, a professional artist, currently resides in Halifax, where she oversees multiple art workshops emphasizing painting, fostering teamwork, and facilitating connections among artists from diverse cultural backgrounds and a wide age range spanning from children to seniors. Additionally, she holds the position of president at the Atlantic Spinners Art and Dance Society, actively organizing numerous events to support local artists. At the age of 16, she left her birthplace, Iran, and later obtained an art degree in Spain, extensively traveling before eventually settling in Canada in 1984. Throughout her life, Mahnaz has nurtured a deep passion for art, which originated in her childhood, and she remains committed to expressing profound emotions and sentiments through every artwork she creates.

She regularly donates paintings to the Breast Cancer Society of Nova Scotia; and participates in the annual 'Paint the Town' fundraiser in Annapolis Royal. She also donates paintings to the Red Cross, UNICEF, and more.
A lot of Mahnaz's inspiration in art comes from travelling, nature, and photography. She specializes in pastels, oil paintings, acrylic painting, and pencil art.
